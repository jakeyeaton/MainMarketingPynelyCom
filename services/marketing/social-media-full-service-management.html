Facebook Management

Facebook is a data-driven platform that allows your business to laser target buyers. We create engaging content to increase your page likes, post reach, website traffic, and leads.

Twitter Management

Twitter is a micro-blogging platform which allows your business to have 1-on-1 conversations with your target audience. We post shareable content to help establish new relationships with new buyers and generate an increase in website traffic and leads.

Instagram Management

Instagram is a photo sharing platform that allows you to tell your brand's story through images and short videos. It currently produces the most content engagement for our clients than any platform. We only use high-quality images and normally post 1-2 time on a daily basis.

The Way It Works



Step 1) Fill Out Our Sign Up Form

Click on the appropriate "Get Started" button to begin the process of signing up. After you click on a Get Started button you will be directed to a Google Form where we ask you for information regarding you, your business, your needs, and service specific information.

Step 2) Taking of Payment

We will send you a link to our ChargeBee account in order to take payment details on a secure page and complete your order. We accept Visa, Mastercard, AMAX, Discover, JCB, Diners Club, PayPal and Amazon Payments.

Step 3) Allow Access To Your Social Media Accounts

Within a day you'll be asked to provide us with access to your social media accounts so we can start our service. We will provide a step-by-step guide on how to give access.

Step 4) Give Access To Pynely Reports, Client Slack Channel and Private Social Media Communities

We will give you access to our dashboard application (Pynely Reports) for real-time reporting and invite you to our client Slack channel plus our Facebook Group for clients and Google+ Community for clients (if wanted).

Step 5) Sit Back and Enjoy. We Are Now Starting To Post For Your Social Media Accounts

Within 2-3 business days of gaining access to your social media accounts, we'll begin social media profile audits, competitive analysis, creating a website/mobile strategy and other work. Then after our "starter" work is complete we will begin full management of your social media accounts. Now it's time for you to focus on the parts of your business that you need to most.

Step 6) Weekly or Monthly Reporting and Constant Communication

We will now start creating/sending your reports on a weekly or monthly basis. While at the same time staying in constant contact via email, Slack, Messenger, Google+, Facebook, Twitter or live chat on our website.

General Questions:

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans.

Do I need to enter my credit card details to sign up?

Yes. After filling our our signup form we will send you a link to our ChargeBee page and this is where you will enter your credit card details, PayPal, or Amazon Payments details on a secure page.

Can I cancel my account at any time?

Yes. If you ever decide that Pynely isn't the best social media marketing solution for your business, simply contact us as we will cancel your account.

How long are your contracts?

All Pynely plans are month to month unless you sign up for an annual plan.

Do you offer any discounted plans?

Yes. We offer a 15% discount on an annual plan when paid upfront.

Can I change my plan later on?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.

What forms of payment do you accept?

You can purchase Pynely with any major credit card, PayPal, or Amazon Payments.

Will I be charged sales tax?

Yes. State and local sales tax will be applied to all fees.

Are my business details private?

Yes. We never share your business details with any third party and only keep your business details between you and us.

How secure is Pynely?

Protecting the data you trust is our first priority. Pynely uses physical, procedural, and technical safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of your information. We regularly backup your data to prevent data loss and aid in recovery. Additionally, we only use Full SSL certificates which gives support for all browsers, even older browsers with no SNI support and access restrictions to our servers to better protect your information, and work with third party security researchers to ensure our practices are secure.

Is Pynely software?

No. We offer social media marketing services made and performed by humans! Byt we're curently working on software to help small business and entrepreneurs to build their social media marketing and outbound sales efforts. If you would like updates for our software and tools we're currently creating feel free to signup to our company newsletter.
