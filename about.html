Welcome to Pynely!

So What is Pynely?

We are a inbound social media marketing agency born out of Seattle, Wa in the winter of 2016. Even though we are born out of Seattle, Wa we choose to work remotely which gives us more freedom to travel, live our lives outside of just work, and hire people from all over the world and walks of life.

We provide services such as helping to build social media marketing strategies, provide social media ads management, full social media account management, content posting services, inbound marketing, content marketing, email marketing and more... If this sounds interesting feel free to drop us a line!

Simple and Straightforward

We're huge believers in the power of simplicity. Every bet, design, marketing campaign, status update, or line of code we make is based on a simple question. How does our work make the life of our clients simpler?

We don't even use huge complicated software to run any part of our business either! In fact, while most companies like ours use feature filled and huge software to run their businesses we pride ourselves to only use a few small apps listed below!

  • Evernote
  • G-Suite
  • Zaiper
  • Github
  • ZenHub
  • Netlify
  • Buffer
  • Drip
  • Drift
  • Pipedrive
  • Zendesk
  • Wave (Accounting)

This gives us more simplicity throughout our workflows and helps increase our productivity towards servicing our clients in a more dedicated fashion.

What We Care About

We are huge believers in business 101. Instead of searching for large amounts of funding to then hope to find a way to earn money for our "business" before time runs out (or our bank account really). We only spend money that we earn and we have on hand. We are in business for the long-haul and not just to "exit".

Businesses are more than just a means to make cool products and money. They also have the power to cause real change in the world and affect the lives of a few people all the way to billions. This is why we always choose to treat people the way we would want to be treated, which is why we always invest a heavy amount of time and resources into our customer support and invest in the simplicity of our products and services. We treat each other like family, and we reward ourselves to having a life outside of work and a good nights sleep.

Always At Your Service
