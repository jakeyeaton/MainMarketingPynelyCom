So Why Facebook PPC?


Facebook has a user base larger than the population of China! How many? 1.49 billion members worldwide, which generates 22 billion ad clicks per year as of the time of this writing! It’s easy to see that Facebook is providing businesses with the largest advertising opportunity since search. Your audience is on there. Somewhere. It’s just a matter of finding them (which is what we do best).

Facebook also provides businesses with tons of different ad types and much like video ads, the level of granularity you can get with Facebook targeting capabilities is insane!!! You can literally target President Trump or The Rock! You can dig very deep with targeting capabilities such as behaviors, interests, demographics, connections, age ranges, languages, devices, locations and much more.

Want to know the craziest part about Facebook ads? This is just the tip of the iceberg!!
