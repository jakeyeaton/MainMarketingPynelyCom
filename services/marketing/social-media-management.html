Social Media Management Service






What To Expect With Pynely


Small Businesses have a million things to do daily. Social media doesn't have to be one. This is where we come in.

We provide almost everything under the sun when it comes to social media marketing. Some of the basic services which are included in most packages are content development, content management, content posting, daily implementation and publishing and monitoring, competitive analysis, full dashboard and monthly reporting, Facebook ad management, and much more...

This is just the basics often clients bundle this service with Facebook application development, promotion and contest implementation, influencer identification and outreach strategy, fan growth, advertisements on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, and/or LinkedIn, and a bunch of other things that make sense for your brand and goals.

Everything is built and managed to the needs of each individual client. The core foundation of our entire process for every single service is rooted in listening first, speaking second and always respecting your brand. Each business has different objectives, different messages, different philosophies, different and unique brands, different target markets. Each business is overall different from each other. We will always respect this.

Facebook Management

Facebook is a data-driven platform that allows your business to laser target buyers. We create engaging content to increase your page likes, post reach, website traffic, and leads.

Twitter Management

Twitter is a micro-blogging platform which allows your business to have 1-on-1 conversations with your target audience. We post shareable content to help establish new relationships with new buyers and generate an increase in website traffic and leads.

Instagram Management

Instagram is a photo sharing platform that allows you to tell your brand's story through images and short videos. It currently produces the most content engagement for our clients than any platform. We only use high-quality images and normally post 1-2 times on a daily basis.

    Google+, Pinterest, LinkedIn and Facebook Groups

We also manage platforms such as Google+, Pinterest, LinkedIn and Facebook Groups and while each platform is different. The concept is the still the same. We create/find great content, engage your target audience, help grow your followers to increase awareness, website traffic, and sales.




How It Works

Step 1) Taking of Payment

Click on the appropriate "Get Started" button to begin the process of signing up. After you click on a Get Started button you will be directed to our Chargify page in order to take payment details on a secure page and complete your order.

Be sure to answer any and all questions you can on this page as it will speed the start-up process. Also, note that we give a 7 day free trial for the first month in order to all gather information and implement our work without wasting your first months worth of our service. This way when you're billed your first month we really worked the whole first month!

We accept Visa, Mastercard, AMAX, Discover, JCB and Diners Club. Alright now, what? After payment is complete you will then be directed to a Google Form where.... (look below!)

Step 2) Fill Our Our Consulting Sign Up Form

We will ask you for information regarding you, your business, your needs, and service specific information. Put in mind some of these questions might require some thought so make sure you either fill out this form when you have a little spare time or please save it with whatever method you use to save web pages with.

We will remind you after three days of not receiving your Google form if it hasn't been received.

Step 3) Information Agreement and NDA signed.

Becuase of the nature of our business where we will need access to your social media accounts and on rare occasion other sensitive business information which you might not want to be shared. Some of the sensitive information we will need access to are but not limited to passwords, email addresses, upcoming sales/marketing campaigns, etc.

So because we are asking for this type of information in order for us to do our job we ask that you sign a Non-disclosure agreement (NDA) which outlines what information we are allowed to use while we are working with you. Because it's under an NDA your information will always remain private and once our agreement has expired we will destroy all sensitive information on our part.

Step 4) Allow Access To Your Social Media Accounts

Within a day you'll be asked to provide us with access to your social media accounts so we can start our service. We will provide a step-by-step guide on how to give access for each social service if needed.

Step 5) Give Access To Pynely Reports

We will give you access to our dashboard application (Pynely Reports) for real-time reporting. We will also provide monthly reporting via email.

Step 6) Sit Back and Enjoy. We Are Now Starting To Post For Your Social Media Accounts

Within 2-3 business days of gaining access to your social media accounts, we'll begin social media profile audits, social media profile creation, competitive analysis, creating a website/mobile strategy and other work. Then after our "starter" work is complete we will begin advertising services, community monitoring, and content creation and posting. Now it's time for you to focus on the parts of your business that you need to most.

Step 7) Weekly or Monthly Reporting with Constant Communication

We will now start creating/sending your reports on a monthly basis. While at the same time staying in constant contact via email, Messenger, Twitter and/or live chat on our website.

Frequently asked questions
(FAQ)

These are questions we hear a lot and you'll find the ansers below. But if you have others, please don't hesitate to contact us - we're here to help





General Questions:

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans.

Do I need to enter my credit card details to sign up?

Yes. After filling out our signup form we will send you a link to our Chargify payment page and this is where you will enter your credit card details on a secure page.

Can I cancel my account at any time?

Yes. If you ever decide that Pynely isn't the best social media marketing solution for your business, simply contact us as we will cancel your account.

How long are your contracts?

All Pynely plans are month to month.

Do you offer any discounted plans?

Not at the moment. Although these are in the pipeline ;)

Can I change my plan later on?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.

What forms of payment do you accept?

You can purchase Pynely with any major credit card, PayPal and Bitcoin by request.

Will I be charged sales tax?

Not currently. Although this might change.

Are my business details private?

Yes. We never share your business details with any third party and only keep your business details between you and us.

How secure is Pynely?

Protecting the data you trust is our first priority. Pynely uses physical, procedural, and technical safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of your information. We regularly backup your data to prevent data loss and aid in recovery. Additionally, we only use Full SSL certificates which gives support for all browsers, even older browsers with no SNI support and access restrictions to our servers to better protect your information, and work with third party security researchers to ensure our practices are secure.

Is Pynely software?

No. We offer social media marketing services made and performed by humans! But we're currently working on software to help small business and entrepreneurs to build their social media marketing and outbound sales efforts. If you would like updates for our software and tools we're currently creating feel free to signup to our company newsletter.



Full Service Management Questions:

How will you know what to post for my company?

Once we receive your order, we will assign a dedicated Content Specialist to help research your industry, competitors, target audience, and anything we feel would help you stand out from the sea of content. This helps ensure that by the time your first post hits social media, your Content Specialist will know just what your audience is looking for which is one of the mina keys to effectively win at social media.

Can I still post on my profiles?

Yes. Your social media accounts belong to you. You are involved as much or as little as you'd like. We do ask if you can contact us before posting in order to keep us up to date.

What exactly do I get with a social media profile audit?

We will go though your profile photos, settings, and layout of your social media accounts and create a new cover photo (if needed), settings and reconfigure your layout to best suit your social media goals.

Is there a limit for the amount of extra platforms I can add to my account?

No. There is no limit to a number of extra platforms you can add to your account as long as they're all associated with the same brand. If you would like to add extra platforms for a different brand you will need to purchase an extra plan. If you need help with this or have any questions feel free to contact us.

What if I have specific products, promotions, or specials I want you to promote?

Simple send us as email what you'd like us to share for you and we'll pass it along to your Content Specialist. You can email specific instructions to hello@pynely.com anytime, and we'll do whatever we can to get them implated ASAP.
