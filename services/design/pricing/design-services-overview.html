No, we do not allow revisions after final delivery

What will I get with my logo design?

The image files you need to use your logo both online and in print. This means you'll get the original, editable vector file (usually an Adobe Illustrator file), vector EPS files in both CMYK and RGB (for print and web, respectively), and a web preview file, like a JPG on PNG, so you can preview your logo on virtually any device.

Plus, you'll get the full legal copyright, so the design will be all yours.

Can I use my own images in my design?

Yes. If you have any specific images (like symbols or icons) that need to be included with your logo, please submit them to our designers with your brief via Basecamp.

What if I need additional logo variations?

We offer extra logo variations for $125 each. If you are in need of additional logo variations feel free to contact us via email or Basecamp.

Who owns what copyright and when?

Once your logo is completed and delivered. You own the copyright 100%. We do require your consent to allow us to display your logo in our portfolio on our website, mobile device, messaging bot, Dribbble, Behance, Pinterest, Instagram, Facebook, Google+, and Tumblr.
