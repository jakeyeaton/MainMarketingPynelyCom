General Questions:

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our Inbound Marketing plans.

Do I need to enter my credit card details to sign up?

Yes. After filling out our signup form we will send you a link to our Chargify payment page and this is where you will enter your credit card details on a secure page.

Can I cancel my account at any time?

Yes. If you ever decide that Pynely isn't the best inbound marketing solution for your business, simply contact us as we will cancel your account.

How long are your contracts?

All Pynely plans are month to month.

Do you offer any discounted plans?

Not at the moment. Although these are in the pipeline ;)

Can I change my plan later on?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.

What forms of payment do you accept?

You can purchase Pynely with any major credit card. PayPal and Bitcoin by request.

Will I be charged sales tax?

Not currently. Although this might change.

Are my business details private?

Yes. We never share your business details with any third party and only keep your business details between you and us.

How secure is Pynely?

Protecting the data you trust is our first priority. Pynely uses physical, procedural, and technical safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of your information. We regularly backup your data to prevent data loss and aid in recovery. Additionally, we only use Full SSL certificates which give support for all browsers, even older browsers with no SNI support and access restrictions to our servers to better protect your information, and work with third-party security researchers to ensure our practices are secure.

Is Pynely software?

No. We offer digital marketing and design services made which are performed by humans! But we're currently working on software and messaging bots to help small business and entrepreneurs to build their social media marketing and inbound sales efforts. If you would like updates for our software and tools we're currently creating feel free to signup to our Pynely Life Newsletter.



Inbound Marketing Service Questions:

How will you know what to post for my company?

Once we receive your order, we will assign a dedicated Content Specialist to help research your industry, competitors, target audience, and anything we feel would help you stand out from the sea of content. This helps ensure that by the time your first post hits social media, your Content Specialist will know just what your audience is looking for which is one of the main keys to effectively win at social media.

Can I still post on my profiles?

Yes. Your social media accounts belong to you. You are involved as much or as little as you'd like. We do ask if you can contact us before posting in order to keep us up to date.

What exactly do I get with a social media profile audit?

We will go through your profile photos, settings, and layout of your social media accounts and create a new cover photo (if needed), settings and reconfigure your layout to best suit your social media goals.

Is there a limit for the amount of extra platforms I can add to my account?

No. There is no limit to a number of extra platforms you can add to your account as long as they're all associated with the same brand. If you would like to add extra platforms for a different brand you will need to purchase an extra plan. If you need help with this or have any questions feel free to contact us.

What if I have specific products, promotions, or specials I want you to promote?

Simply send us as email what you'd like us to share on your different marketing channels and we'll pass it along to your Content Specialist. You can email specific instructions to hello@pynely.com anytime, and we'll do whatever we can to get them implemented ASAP.

How do I know if Inbound Marketing is a good fit for my company?

If your company is looking to generate leads from your online marketing, has a sales team to work those leads, and is willing to think creatively, we're probably a good fit for you. We have experience working with all types of lead generating companies, including SaaS and technology companies, business services and consultants, home contractors, retirement communities, and fitness companies. We understand that we're not a fit for everyone, and if we're not completely confident that we can help you succeed with inbound marketing, we'll let you know that upfront.

What are my obligations when working with Pynely?

We work with clients that are very involved and client's who are not. At a minimum, we request that clients attend scheduled meetings, provide us access to the platforms, people, and materials we need to complete quality work, and review work in a timely manner. A typical Pynely client will allocate 5-10 hours per week working with us.

Can Pynely's inbound marketing services be customized based on our internal team's capabilities?

Yes! Our model allows complete flexibility so you'll be able to get the help you need to succeed. If your internal team is already doing a great hob in particular areas on inbound marketing, we'll use that as a starting point and allocate points where we can make the biggest impact in your marketing.

What type of ROI should I expect?

It's common for people think that inbound marketing alone will generate an immediate spike in leads. Although this can happen if a campaign goes "viral", which is rare, inbound marketing is a long-term play.

Inbound marketing is opposite of advertising. Effective advertising, which is often much more expensive than inbound, can generate that spike in results, however, once you turn it off, the results go away.

Inbound marketing is more like investing in the stock marketing, where results compound with more content you release and flowers, subscribers, and database contacts you build. Also like investing in stocks, your investment will continue to produce returns even if you stop contributing since past content will continue to get found in search engines, generating traffic and leads for years to come.

It may be a few months before you can start seeing a true ROI from inbound marketing, but the long-term benefits will power your future sales efforts and can significantly increase the value of your business.

A recent HubSpot study reports that 83% of respondents saw a measurable increase in lead generation within 7 months, and 65% reported seeing a measurable increase after only 4 months. Remember, performance gets significantly better over time.
