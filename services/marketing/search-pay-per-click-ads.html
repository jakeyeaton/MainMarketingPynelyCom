What To Expect While Working With Pynely

Below is just a small amount of PPC management services that will be addressed and worked upon on a frequent basis for your campaigns. We're not one of the "trendy" PPC companies who burns clients and only works on their campaigns once every 2 weeks. We want to keep you around. Because at the end of the day. When you win, we win.

Improving CTR and average ad position

Creating Multiple Adgroups by analyzing different products/services offered by the client

Create multiple effective Ads for every Adgroup and tuning the ads for performance

Decreasing the monthly spending budget for existing PPC campaigns by analyzing and improving the Quality Score

Suggesting/implementing improvements for Landing page optimization for specific Adgroups

Reducing client's monthly spend by removing irrelevant keywords from the search query keywords, a continuous process of adding/removing positive and negative keywords.

Bidding analysis and management down to the keyword level and reducing CPC

Analyzing your industry, understanding the end user needs/wants and creating effective keywords from users' search points

Features

Initial Consultation

We'll discuss your project, target audience, top competitors and campaign goals.

Google Analytics

We want to make sure all of your Google Analytics is set-up properly as well. So we will go ahead and tie that all together for you.

Keyword Selection

This applies to both keywords we want to go after and keywords that we want to negative out so Google never shows us ads for them.

GEO Targeting

Will your campaign be local, nation or international? We can target based on states, cities, counties, zip codes, etc

Landing Page Design

Yes we do build landing pages. We will help consult any in-house designer you have on payroll in the design aspects of what we need at no additional charge, but if you need us to build out a page for you it will be an additional cost.

Phone Call Tracking

If you are in a lead generation campaign we will optimize your campaign/landing page to drive phone calls as well.

Ad Copywriting

Our ads have ridiculous CTR rates which in turn leads to higher quality scores and overall a more optimal performing campaign.

Bid Management

This is simple which is hard to understand for us why most companies need all kinds of fancy tools and gizmos to do this. We decrease or drop bids on loser keywords and increase on winners.

Support

We understand things break sometimes and the internet and tech can be overwhelming. We are always here for support.

Finance

We understand that you may not have what we quote you all upfront but can make it work month to month. This is why before we even start work we offer flexible financing options by accepting major Credit/Debit cards, Amazon Payments, PayPal, and even Bitcoin. (Apple Pay and Android Pay is coming soon).

Reports

We give reports on key data points for each service we are providing weekly or monthly (you decide). We give these reports by dashboards for different services (currently working on a one dashboard solution).

Simplicity

Simplicity is at the root of how we run everything at Pynely. We don't like when thing are complicated and we make sure everything we do is as simple as possible for both you and ourselves.
