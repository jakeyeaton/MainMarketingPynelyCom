What To Expect While Working With Pynely

Small Businesses have a million things to do daily. Social media shouldn't be one. This is where we come in.

As more brands establish themselves and engage on social platforms, organic reach and impressions have greatly suffered. The reason for this is because there are 1,000,000 businesses and people at the same time yelling “Hey look over here and buy this” or “Hey look how great we are” which has caused customers to find it easy to develop an automatic anti-promotion filter when scrolling through there Facebook feed. Unfortunately this is a tread that we feel is going to only strengthen on every social media platform. So the only solution for businesses is to not be completely emerged as yet another yelling voice in the sea of content but rather cut in line directly to their customers using social advertisements.

But using social ads isn’t as simple as clicking the “Boost Post” button (unless you’re okay with lighting your money on fire) and calling it a day. The Facebook advertisement platform as become a highly sophisticated tool which offers over a dozen advertising objectives, many advertisement types, as well as countless ways to place and optimize your ads. We have spent the past year dissecting the ins and outs of this platform and have the expertise to back it up.

It doesn’t matter if your goal is increasing web conversions, raising awareness for an event, driving traffic to a website or mobile app, generate leads, or simply growing your social audiences. We have the skills and tools to help optimize your ad dollars. But while Twitter might help boost your overall presense for in the moment marketing it might not always be the most optimal solution for your business. Be sure to keep an open mind with other platforms such as Instagram, Facebook , and LinkedIn which offer a more targeted audience from the start. If you would like to find out more about these options feel free to contact us.

Step 4) Give Access To Pynely Reports and client Slack Channel

We will give you access to our dashboard application (Pynely Reports) for real-time reporting and invite you to our client Slack channel plus our Facebook Group for clients and Google+ Community for clients (if wanted).

Step 5) Site back and enjoy. We Are Now Starting The Management of Your Instagram PPC Campaigns.

Within 2-3 business days of gaining access to your social media accounts, we'll begin the management your Instagram PPC campaigns.

Step 6) Weekly or Monthly Reporting and Constant Communication

We will now start creating/sending your reports on a weekly or monthly basis. While at the same time staying in constant contact via email, Slack, Messenger, Google+, Facebook, Twitter or live chat on our website.

General Questions:

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans.

Do I need to enter my credit card details to sign up?

Yes. After filling our our signup form we will send you a link to our ChargeBee page and this is where you will enter your credit card details, PayPal, or Amazon Payments details on a secure page.

Can I cancel my account at any time?

Yes. If you ever decide that Pynely isn't the best social media marketing solution for your business, simply contact us as we will cancel your account.

How long are your contracts?

All Pynely plans are month to month unless you sign up for an annual or quarterly plan.

Do you offer any discounted plans?

Yes. We offer a 15% discount on an annual plan and 10% on quarterly plans when paid upfront.

Can I change my plan later on?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.

What forms of payment do you accept?

You can purchase Pynely with any major credit card, PayPal, or Amazon Payments.

Will I be charged sales tax?

Yes. State and local sales tax will be applied to all fees.

Are my business details private?

Yes. We never share your business details with any third party and only keep your business details between you and us.
