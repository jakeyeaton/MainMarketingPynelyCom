What To Expect With This Service

Establishing and maintaining a presence on social media can be very difficult and finding the hours or the knowledge to know-how to grow a brand on social media is very challenging especially for busy entrepreneurs and small businesses.

This is why Pynely offers a social media content management service to help busy entrepreneurs and small businesses like you maintain and grow a solid presence on social media, this is so when your customers and potential customers search for you, you’re there.

Where they look which is social media. But just being there isn’t enough with the ever growing social media presence some businesses have, you need to be there consistently, establishing your relevance, credibility, and approachability.

The Way It Works

1) Fill Out Our Sign Up & Payment Form (Takes less than five minutes)

Click on the appropriate Get Started button to begin the process. First, you’ll fill out our Sign Up Form, then you’ll enter your payment details on a secure page and complete your order. Second, check your inbox right away for your Getting Started email and confirm your order.

2) Allow Access To Your Social Media Accounts

Within a day you’ll be asked to provide us with access to your social media accounts so we can start posting.

3) Sit back and enjoy. We Are Now Starting To Post.

Within 2-3 business days of gaining access to your social media accounts, we’ll begin posting content to your pages. Now it’s time to focus on the parts of your business that you need to most.


Common FAQ's

1. Who will be working on my social media accounts?

We assign a locally based in the United States, well-trained, dedicated Content Specialist to research your industry, discover, and share content for your accounts. Our Content Specialists are highly focused on Social Media Content. They spend hours searching the web and many resources to find the most relevant content to share on your accounts. Our Content Specialist’s main goal is to engage with your audience in just the way you would if you had the time. We’re firm believers in full transparency and ownership of our work, and it will show in everything we do.

2. How will you know what to post for my company?

Once we receive your order, we will assign a dedicated Content Specialist to help research your industry, competitors, target audience, and anything we feel would help you stand out from the pack. This helps ensure that by the time your first post hits social media, your Content Specialist will know just what your audience is looking for which is one of the main key’s to effectively win at social media.

3. Can I still post on my own profiles?

Yes, your social media accounts belong to you. You are involved as much or as little as you’d like.

4. What if I have specific products, promotions, or specials I want you to promote?

Simply send us an email or message over Messenger or Slack with what you’d like us to share for you, and we’ll pass it along to your Content Specialist. You can email specific instructions to hello@pynely.com anytime, and we’ll do whatever we can to get them implanted ASAP.

5. Do I have to sign a contract?

We are a little old school in this sense where we have a core belief of earning your business month after month, quarter after quarter, or year after year. There is no contract involved. You can cancel anytime you’d like. Just know what after your 14-Day Money Back Guarantee there are no returns on our service.
