Pynely vs 99DollarSocial


Hey there! Thinking about moving over to Pynely?

Tired of receiving bad content?

Join us on a world changing, earth shattering, epic journey of engaging dialogue as we hand over the facts to help you give an overview of Pynely vs 99DollarSocial compared.




1) Real Social Media Marketing Strategy


What's the point in posting content if nobody reads it, or worse it doesn't lead to any new leads?

Unlike 99DollarSocial who will just fill your social media accounts with content for the sake of filling in content. We make sure to create a real strategy behind your content that we create and share in order to achieve an end goal.

These goals could be lead generation, brand recognition, community engagement, sales nurturing, etc or a combination of all.





2) Pynley Can Plug Right Into Your SEO and Larger Digital Marketing Strategy


Social media is just one piece of a larger puzzle called digital marketing and we make sure our solutions plug right into your overall digital marketing strategy.

Because of this, we will work with your digital marketing team, another agency, you or help you create a larger digital marketing strategy.

We do this by creating a collaborative workspace via Basecamp 3 where we share files, ideas, work, and communicate about what we're working on and how it's going.





3) High-Quality Content


When 99DollarSocial share content the heavy majority of it is curating content from others. Which can drive a little bit of traffic and does build some credibility on your end. But it doesn't last long. The reason? You're sharing content that isn't yours. When a follower clicks on the link it goes to someone else's website and not yours.

How does it help you generate more sales and leads if 99DollarSocial sends them all to another store or website?

The answer is "IT DOESN'T HELP YOU". You know what does help you? Creating high-quality content which tells your businesses story and generates leads.

This is what we do at Pynely. We create high-quality content with the goal of whatever your business's goals are.





4) Dedicated Social Media Marketing Team


What if I told you the secret to a lot of social media agencies who sell services at an affordable price is the fact that all of their social media managers manage multiple clients at once?

Their minds are not 100% dedicated to your content or brand but rather only dedicated when they look at their Google Calendar and see "oh for this hour I have to care about XYZ client's content and brand".

There are some social media marketing teams that manage over 30 brands per week! When do you ever have time to think about how to effectively sell your client's brand when you're busy thinking of 29 other brands?

I don't think you would feel so comparable giving these people money and the responsibility of representing your brand and nor do we. This is why we installed a limit of three clients per social media marketing team which they are allowed to manage at once.





5) Detailed Reports & Monthly Breakdowns


A lot of agencies offer only monthly reporting on simple metrics (that frankly don't matter much) such as the number of followers, likes, and comments via email.

We don't do that.

We offer a full dashboard app in which you can log in, view and download current metrics about your social media accounts.

As well as a monthly report outlining varies work such a key social media metrics, what worked and didn't work, what we found which could be applied towards your current marketing strategy, what we are planning on trying differently and what we plan on accomplishing and how we will do it for the upcoming month.





The Skinny.


The short version

Pynely is better for serious businesses. 99DollarSocial is better for hobbyists.

Pynely is best if...

  • - You care about the quality of content shared on your social media profiles
  • - Driving sales and leads are more important than just sharing curated articles from random sources
  • - You're looking for a full circle social media marketing solution that can plug right into your exciting marketing strategy

99DollarSocial may be better choice when...

  • - You really are too busy or lazy to share random articles on your social media accounts
  • - The quality of content isn't important to your brand
  • - You strongly feel as if social media isn't that important
