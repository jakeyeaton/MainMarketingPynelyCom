Frequently asked questions
(FAQ)

These are questions we hear a lot and you'll find the ansers below. But if you have others, please don't hesitate to contact us - we're here to help



Can I outsource communication to Pynely?

It is definitely possible for us to handle the entire communication with the end-client. However, it would require a different pricing as the amount of work required is substantial. We typically find the communication pattern that matches your clients needs and we will provide you with a custom quote.

Is there a minimum of acounts required to outsource communication?

If we were to take over communication, we would require a minimum of 50 accounts to enable White Label communication. This includes new emails with your domain and aliases to make it seem like our representative are from your agency.

Who handles the communication with the end client?

Communication with the end client will be handled by reps from your company. We will not contact your client or participate in conference calls. White Label communication will be between representatives from your agency and PPC Strategists from our team.

How are my accounts going to be managed within Pynely?

We are strong believers in helping our social media Strategists focus on fewer verticals and growing their strength within those verticals exponentially. Your accounts will mainly be managed by the same team within Pynely.

The individual social media accounts will be managed by different people within that team depending on how their skills and experience match your client's business. In rate cases, we might involve another team if a new account drastically diers from your regular clients.
