What To Expect While Working With Pynely

Small Businesses have a million things to do daily. Social media shouldn't be one. This is where we come in.

As more brands establish themselves and engage on social platforms, organic reach and impressions have greatly suffered. The reason for this is because there are 1,000,000 businesses and people at the same time yelling “Hey look over here and buy this” or “Hey look how great we are” which has caused customers to find it easy to develop an automatic anti-promotion filter when scrolling through there Facebook feed. Unfortunately this is a tread that we feel is going to only strengthen on every social media platform. So the only solution for businesses is to not be completely emerged as yet another yelling voice in the sea of content but rather cut in line directly to their customers using social advertisements.

But using social ads isn’t as simple as clicking the “Boost Post” button (unless you’re okay with lighting your money on fire) and calling it a day. The Facebook advertisement platform as become a highly sophisticated tool which offers over a dozen advertising objectives, many advertisement types, as well as countless ways to place and optimize your ads. We have spent the past year dissecting the ins and outs of this platform and have the expertise to back it up.

It doesn’t matter if your goal is increasing web conversions, raising awareness for an event, driving traffic to a website or mobile app, generate leads, or simply growing your social audiences. We have the skills and tools to help optimize your ad dollars. But while Twitter might help boost your overall presense for in the moment marketing it might not always be the most optimal solution for your business. Be sure to keep an open mind with other platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, and LinkedIn which offer a more targeted audience from the start. If you would like to find out more about these options feel free to contact us.


Features

Geo-Targeting

We will figure our the geographical area which would work best for your advertisement.

Ad-Type

We will help with determining which ad-type would work the best for your advertisement campaign to drive the best ROI.

Demographic Targeting

If you have a demographic in which you are seeking to target in mind we will use that demographic. But if you don't we offer to help with determining a demographic which would we believe would drive the best results using our highly targeted tools and software.

Delivery Optimizaiton

More often than not there are many times when you shouldn’t advertise. For example launching an eCommerce sale the same week as Amazon Prime day will not only not be the best idea but will also result in costing you far more than if you wait a week. We will help determine when is the best time to deliver your advertisement campaign.

Full Reporting (Pynely Reports)

We provide weekly/bi-weekly/monthly reports (your preference) via Slack, Messenger, and/or Email and give access to Pynely Reports so you can view data points as they happen in real time. The platforms which are avaiable on Pynely Reports are Facebook, Twitter, Instagram LinkedIn and YouTube.

Lookalike Audiences

Wouldn’t it be nice to kill two birds with one stone with your advertisement campaign? (Well not literally kill birds) This is what lookalike audiences help do for you. By intense research, we can determine audiences which look like another audience. This results (most of the time) in a major increase of ROI for your advertisement campaign. Think of it as getting a 2 for 1 deal on your ads.

Support

We understand things break sometimes and the internet and tech can be overwhelming. We are always here for support.

Finance

Reports

We give reports on key data points for each service we are providing weekly or monthly (you decide). We give these reports by whichever method works best for you email, Slack, Messenger or our dashboard applicaiton.

Simplicity

Simplicity is at the root of how we run everything at Pynely. We don't like when things are complicated and we make sure everything we do is as simple as possible for both you and ourselves.
