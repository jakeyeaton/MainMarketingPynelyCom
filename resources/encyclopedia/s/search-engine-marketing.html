Search Engine Marketing (SEM)



Search Engine Marketing (SEM) refers to both paid and organic search engine marketing tactics. Essentially, any tactic that involves driving value for a business using search engines falls into the category of SEM. On the paid side of SEM, you would include Google AdWords, and on the free side, you would include Search Engine Optimization SEO.
